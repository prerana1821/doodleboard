Key Features ✨

Masterful Pencil Sketches

w/ Expressive Precision

Let your creativity flow with our Pencil feature. Sketch, doodle, or diagram with absolute freedom using adjustable size, color, and patterns. Whether you're brainstorming ideas or adding a personal touch to your work, the possibilities are endless.

Highlight Brilliance:

Vibrant Marker Annotations

Make your points stand out with our Marker tool. Highlight key information, underline crucial details, or draw attention to important sections effortlessly. With adjustable size, color, and patterns, your insights will shine brighter than ever.

Error-Free Editing:

Wipe Away Mistakes with Ease

Say goodbye to errors with our Eraser feature. Correct mistakes seamlessly and refine your creations with precision. With adjustable size, no mistake is too big or too small to fix.

Shape Your Vision

with Precision Shapes

Transform your ideas into polished creations with our Shapes feature. From circles to triangles, unlock a world of possibilities with a variety of shapes at your disposal. Perfect for diagrams, illustrations, and more.

Text That Speaks Volumes

w/ Dynamic Typography

Let your words do the talking with our Text tool. Add captions, labels, or commentary with customizable size and font options. With draggable, resizable, and delete functionalities, your message will always be on point.

Playful Sticky Notes:

with Colorful Annotations

Make your annotations pop with our Sticky Note feature. Add colorful notes, reminders, or comments to your canvas with ease. With draggable and resizable options, organizing your thoughts has never been easier.

Upload & Annotate:

Bring Your Images to Life

Elevate your images with our Upload feature. Upload photos or graphics directly to your canvas and annotate them seamlessly. With draggable and resizable options, customization is key to making your visuals truly yours.

Background Color

Set the Stage for Your Creativity

Create the perfect backdrop for your creations with our Background Color feature. Personalize your canvas with customizable background colors that set the mood and enhance your artwork.

Undo/Redo & Reset

Stay in Control of Your Creations

Take control of your creative process with our Undo/Redo and Reset functionalities. Experiment freely, knowing that you can always backtrack or start fresh with just a click.

Download & Share

Spread Your Creativity Far & Wide

Share your creations with the world with our Download and Share feature. Export your masterpieces effortlessly, ensuring they reach the right audience every time. Whether it's a professional presentation or a personal project, sharing has never been easier.
